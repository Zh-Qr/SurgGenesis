The question

What happens next in the operating room?

SurgGenesis adapts a video foundation model to laparoscopic surgery and rolls observed endoscopic frames forward into plausible future video.

Its progressive, additive LoRA curriculum preserves prior adaptation while adding structured surgical conditioning for triplets and trajectories.

Method

Progressive adaptation for surgical dynamics.

Each stage fuses its learned update into the frozen foundation model before the next, more specialized adaptation begins.

Results

A shared predictive latent for downstream surgery.

Generated video is more than an endpoint: it becomes a reusable substrate for understanding, reconstruction, and forecasting.
